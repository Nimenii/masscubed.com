President Lily Travers (they/he)
Hi everyone! I'm Lily, and I'm honoured to be your 2024 President! You may recognise me as the outgoing First Year Rep for Research. My majors are physics and mathematics, and you'll often find me in the commo working on problems with friends. The 2023 committee set a strong precedent for us to follow, but I'm so excited to jump into the year alongside the wonderful 2024 committee. We are committed to bringing more engaging events, both new and traditional, and I am focused on promoting inclusion of all of the Advanced Science courses, especially Global Challenges and Data Science. I'd love to get to know you better, so please don't hesitate to approach me at events, in the commo, or just around campus. You can also contact me at ltra0060@student.monash.edu if you have any suggestions or just want to have a chat! Can't wait to see you around! Vice President Michael Gitonobel
