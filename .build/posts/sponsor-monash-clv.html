Monash Careers, Leadership and Volunteering

A word from our sponsor

Here’s the situation: you’re nearly finished with your degree, and you’re feeling that tiny bit of stress from looking for a great job that best suits your major.

The solution? Well, it’s Monash University’s own careers and volunteering service! From mock job interviews to fully-fledged presentations on cover-letter writing and employability skills (a must!), everything you need to get the job you want is right at your footsteps. Visit the brand-new office at Clayton Campus Centre, or head over at monash.edu.au/careers.

Useful links

Practice Interview Session: May 4, 2016 at Clatyon, a workshop to help with interviews.

Leap into Leadership Online: a suite of free modules to develop your employability and leadership skills at your own pace. It's available for all current Monash students, any time - so you can complete as many modules as you like, at any stage of your studies.

Volunteering at Monash: Being a volunteer means surrounding yourself with like-minded people, working together to help others. You will meet new friends, network with inspirational mentors, and make unique connections.
