Events

Throughout the year we run many events, from trivia nights to mixers with fellow students or academics.

Upcoming event information:

Completed events:

20/02: Amazing Race

Welcome the new first year students with a race throughout the campus, ending with a BBQ! Find us outside the common room at 19 Rainforest Walk.

08/03: Ordinary General Meeting (OGM)

The first club meeting of the year! Several committee roles are still available, including editor and the first year representative positions. More information, including location, can be found in the official announcement.

14/03: Trivia Night

Team up with your friends (or meet new people!) for one of the most competitive events of the year! Pizza, snacks and drinks will be provided from 6pm. Tickets are available here.

22/04: Wellbeing Day (12-2pm)

Does the semester feels like it's dragging on? Enjoy snacks and games in commo to help ease your mind and relax. Hot drinks, colouring in, face masks and nail painting will be provided!

08/05: Powerpoint Evening

Present whatever powerpoint creation you choose to in a relaxed night with snacks and friends! Whether you choose to present something educational, funny, or blind-swap presentations with a friend, we can't wait to see your creative slideshows. Tickets are available here.

05/08: Academic Mixer

Save the date for our annual academic and industry mixer event! Express your interest here.

22/08: Battle of the Courses

Challenge your friends (and enemies!) from the other advanced science courses in a variety of games and events! May the best course win...

17/09: Science Society Trivia

Our club has come together with MSS, SOCS, SPAM and BioSoc to bring you an evening of general trivia, fun mini games and good times, all inspired by the Olympics! Everyone is welcome to join us on Tuesday 17th of September at Sir John's Bar (Clayton Campus) - food and drink is included with a ticket purchase, and alcohol will also be available to be purchased separately. Doors open at 6pm, tickets available here.

20/09: AGM

Elect a new committee and welcome the new year! Please see the official notice for more details.

30/09: MASS2

2024 brings back the annual MASS2 event in which past and previous students can share their work and catch up over snacks and drinks. Want to present a project or other endeavor? Express your interest here!

17/10: End of Semester BBQ

Join the outgoing committee for a final barbeque event on Thursday October 17th from 11am to celebrate the end of semester! Non-meat options will be avaliable as well as snacks and drinks. Outside Commo!!
